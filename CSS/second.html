Ram's journey, intertwined with Anandhi, is a story that will resonate with readers for a long time. A story that teaches us the power of love, friendship, and the strength of human connection. An emotionally charged, unputdownable novel about love, revenge, loss and friendship that has taken readers by storm... Ram, a native of Alleppey, Kerala, relocates to the bustling city of Chennai with dreams of mastering filmmaking and writing a novel.
